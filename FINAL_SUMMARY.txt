  Example: https://your-backend.herokuapp.com
  Note: No trailing slash!

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  ⚠️ TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN (from @BotFather)
  ⚠️ GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID (from Google Cloud Console)
  ⚠️ GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET (from Google Cloud Console)
  ⚠️ WEBAPP_URL (your Vercel URL)
  ⚠️ REDIRECT_URI (Vercel URL + /callback.html)
